What is the Mining Difficulty?

It’s a mesure of how difficult is to find a hash below the target value (a 256-bit number) during the Proof of Work.

Mining Difficulty

Target Value

How does it work?

More miners join the network

Block creation rate increases

Average mining time decreases

Mining difficulty increases

Block creation rate goes down

Average mining time goes back to normal

The target value is recalculated every 2,016 blocks (aproximately two weeks)

The ideal average mining time is stablished as 10 minutes per block.
